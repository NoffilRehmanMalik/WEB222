Welcome to Toronto's Hidden Gems!

5 unique places and experiences I bet you didn't know about.

Scarborough Bluffs Beach

Scarborough Bluffs Beach in Toronto, Ontario, is a captivating lakeside destination known for its striking cliffs and sandy shoreline. Offering panoramic views of Lake Ontario, it's a popular spot for relaxation, water activities, and hiking along the picturesque bluffs.

Monkey's Paw

Monkey's Paw is a book store like no other in Toronto. This fascinating little nook is home to the Biblio-Mat, a bespoke vending machine that, for $3, gives you a randomly-selected surprise vintage book.

Learn More

Redway Road Staircase

The Redway Road Staircase is a charming urban gem located in Toronto, Ontario. This picturesque staircase offers a delightful shortcut between neighborhoods, surrounded by lush greenery and a tranquil ambiance, making it a popular spot for both locals and visitors seeking a scenic and serene urban escape.

Learn More

Little Tokyo

Little Tokyo, situated in Toronto, is a vibrant cultural enclave known for its rich Japanese heritage. This bustling district offers an array of authentic restaurants, shops, and cultural events, making it a must-visit destination for those looking to immerse themselves in Japanese cuisine and traditions.

Learn More

BATL Axe Throwing

BATL (Backyard Axe Throwing League) in Toronto offers an exhilarating axe-throwing experience for both beginners and seasoned throwers. With expert guidance and a social atmosphere, it's a unique spot to test your skills and have fun in a competitive yet safe environment.
